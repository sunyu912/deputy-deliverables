Design Overview

A serious game built to make life-saving procedures instinctive under stress.

Core Loop

Assess the scene → identify hazards → follow protocol → evacuate & treat survivors → debrief. Each run scores speed, accuracy, and lives saved.

Disaster Scenarios

Building fires, earthquakes, flash floods, chemical (HAZMAT) spills, and structural collapse — each with authentic physics, spreading danger, and time pressure.

Learn by Doing

Real protocols baked into mechanics: Drop-Cover-Hold, RACE & PASS for fire, CPR rhythm, triage tagging, and safe evacuation routing.

Signature Mechanics

  • • Hazard Scanner — spot and neutralize dangers before they escalate
  • • Stress & Oxygen meters — panic and smoke degrade your control
  • • Guide-the-Civilians — herd panicked NPCs to safe exits
  • • First-Aid mini-games — CPR timing, bleeding control, triage
  • • Branching decisions — wrong protocol = real consequences

Training Outcomes

  • • Builds instinctive response through repetition & scoring
  • • Skill badges: Fire, Flood, Earthquake, HAZMAT, First Aid
  • • After-action analytics track reaction time & protocol compliance
  • • Co-op mode for team coordination & role assignment
  • • For schools, offices, first responders & safety drills
